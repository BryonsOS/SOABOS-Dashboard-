# Sick Sales Experiment Log

Track experiments so the team builds learning, not chaos.

| Date | Experiment | Hypothesis | Variant | KPI | Result | Decision |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| YYYY-MM-DD | Homepage hero image swap | Better product-first image increases clicks to PDP | lifestyle image vs flat lay | CTR / CVR | pending | pending |

## Good experiments
- change first product image
- change hero copy
- add sticker bundle framing
- test free shipping threshold callout
- reorder collection by best chance to sell
- swap product title to cleaner buyer language

## Avoid
- changing five things and calling it a test
- running no-control chaos and guessing after
- repeating old losers because nobody logged them

# Sick Sales Offer Calendar

A lightweight planning grid for the next 2 weeks.

## Week structure

### Monday
- Hero SKU reset
- best seller or easiest-win product
- simple sticker attach move

### Tuesday
- social proof / customer proof / popularity angle

### Wednesday
- bundle day
- tee + sticker pack or threshold bonus

### Thursday
- content-led push
- reel/post/story tied to product story or event relevance

### Friday
- urgency push
- weekend sale, low-stock angle, or free sticker threshold

### Weekend
- event energy / lifestyle / community proof
- resurface winner from the week

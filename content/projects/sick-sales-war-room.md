## Why it exists

Money is the job.

If shirts and stickers need to move, the system cannot stay fuzzy. The team needs one place that answers:

- what are we pushing right now
- what product should get the main slot
- what bundle should raise average order value
- what channel should carry the message today
- what numbers matter enough to watch daily
- what experiment is live and whether it worked

## Current attack model

- pick one hero product, not ten equal products
- pair it with one sticker or add-on bundle to lift AOV
- drive one clear angle across site, email, and social the same day
- update a tiny scorecard daily so momentum is visible
- rotate creative based on hooks, not random guessing

## Sales lanes

- Hero SKU: the one shirt or lead product getting the push
- Fast cash add-on: sticker pack, slap, or low-friction extra item
- Bundle move: shirt + sticker, event tee + decal, limited drop + free ship threshold
- Channel pressure: homepage, email, SMS if available, IG feed, IG story, reels, paid if active
- Conversion help: cleaner product naming, tighter first image, urgency, social proof, threshold offers

## Non-negotiables

- one main push at a time
- every campaign needs a hook
- every hook needs a KPI
- if a product is not moving, change the angle, image, placement, or offer quickly
- log experiments so the same bad idea does not get repeated as a new idea

## Morning questions

- What are we selling today?
- Why should someone care today?
- Where will they see it first?
- What add-on increases cart value?
- What number tells us by tonight if it worked?
